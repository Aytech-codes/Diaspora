About Us

In this site people from different cultures can read and learn about others. Such cultural activities may include (but are not limited to) food, dress, principles, sayings, ideas, rules, regulations, values, norms, and more. The list is endless.

It is a site for people who are married across nations and continents to interact and get to understand the cultures of their partners. Our Community Forums

Join our supportive community forums designed to help you navigate life in Scotland. Connect with others who share similar experiences and find the support you need.

Diaspora Families

This forum is a space for families who have arrived or settled in the United Kingdom. Families here will include but not limited to couples and their children as well as single parents and their children. In some cultures, family is broader than the immediate family. We are culturally robust and sensitive as we relate with all groups.

Cultural Transition Support

Guidance on navigating cultural differences and establishing a sense of belonging in your new environment.

Children's Integration

Support for children's school integration and social adjustment to minimize transition challenges.

Community Connection

Connect with other families who understand your journey and can share valuable insights.

Sometimes the children also struggle with school. We have accompanied many families to enable the integration and smooth transition for their children. We encourage unity and understanding in your family as you navigate the challenging time.

So, do not struggle alone. We are here to help.

Diaspora Married Couples

This forum is a space for couples to be enriched and supported as they find their way around their new neighbourhood. Sometimes couples are not aware of the impact of changes in their relationships. This has led to long term challenges. In some cases, it has led to break ups. We seek to mitigate these challenges and we offer the right support right from their entry into their new culture.

Relationship Counseling

Access to professional guidance for navigating relationship challenges in a new cultural context.

Couples Community

Connect with other couples who understand the unique challenges of cross-cultural relationships.

Couples Events

Regular social gatherings and workshops designed to strengthen your relationship.

Our continuous support has enabled cordial relationships and smooth transition in those families. In addition, we offer care and support as required.

Connect With Couples Forum

Diaspora Individuals

This forum is a space for anyone who has recently arrived or has been within the United Kingdom. It is a forum to meet and interact with people who share similar experiences. While the United Kingdom is culturally an independent society, many who have arrived from other cultures are not individualistic in their worldview. Hence, the challenge of finding likeminded people and engaging with them.

Cultural Navigation

Resources and support for understanding and adapting to British culture while maintaining your identity.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with others who share your background or interests to build meaningful relationships.

Local Integration

Guidance on becoming an active member of your local community while honoring your heritage.

This forum provides such community. Please get in touch and contribute as you gain from others as well.

Diaspora Millennials

People seem to be making progress in life as they go about their businesses. And so, life continues, and everyone minds their own business. The world is growing apart at a fast rate. This may be called individualism or maintaining individual space. We seem to mind our individual business, let people go on with their life as we take care of ours. But is that real? Is it true that what others do are none of our concern? Where is such mindset taking us?

Digital Community

Online and in-person opportunities to connect with other millennials in meaningful ways.

Innovation & Ideas

A platform to share and develop ideas that bridge cultural gaps and create positive change.

Work-Life Balance

Support for navigating career development while maintaining cultural connections and personal well-being.

While there are advantages to the evolving culture, the disadvantages seem more. In this forum, we invite millennials in the diaspora for discussion on various topics and issues.

Newcomers Forum

At Diaspora Families Support, our mission is to assist individuals and families relocating to Scotland, providing guidance, education, and resources for smooth integration.
We help newcomers navigate cultural, social, and practical challenges of adapting to life in Scotland.

What We Offer

Transition & Settlement

Guidance, signposting, workshops & seminars on education, jobs, and housing.

Educational Programs

Language/cultural classes & a resource library with legal, health, and education info.

Community Forum

Engage in discussions, share experiences, and connect with a support network.

Cultural Exchange

Food tasting events, cultural exhibitions, and celebrations of diversity.

Cross-Cultural Families

Spaces for cross-cultural marriages & mixed-race families to share experiences.

Child-Friendly Environment

Youth engagement activities & parenting support for cross-cultural families.
